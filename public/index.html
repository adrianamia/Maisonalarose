The Maison

An atelier, not a template factory.

"A good template doesn't call attention to itself — the brand is what shines through it."

Maison à la Rose was born from a simple conviction: the visual tools we use every day deserve the same care as a made-to-measure dress. No generic layouts, no candy-pink gradients — every template is designed as a collector's piece, with its own typography, its own breathing room.

Every template is delivered straight to your inbox after purchase: open the link, personalize it in Canva in minutes, without ever losing its elegance.
